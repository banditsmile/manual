Functions¶

This topic lists the “built-in” functions and operators available in MemSQL queries. They are grouped by type or purpose.

Aggregate functions¶

Aggregate functions are used to calculate and condense values over multiple rows. They are only used in the context of queries with a GROUP BY clause. They are listed below in alphabetical order.

  • APPROX_COUNT_DISTINCT
  • AVG
  • COUNT
  • GROUP_CONCAT
  • MAX
  • MIN
  • STD
  • STDDEV_SAMP
  • SUM
  • VARIANCE
  • VAR_SAMP
